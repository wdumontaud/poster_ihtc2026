Introduction

Design optimization of the insulation domain in a building wall

William Dumontaud LaSIE, UMR 7356 CNRS, La Rochelle Université, 17000 La Rochelle, France · IHTC 2026

This page presents the supplementary animations for the poster displayed at IHTC 2026. The research investigates the shape optimisation of the material interface in multilayer building façades, parameterised using Bézier curves and optimised with the NSGA-II multi-objective genetic algorithm, with application to Brazilian climates.
